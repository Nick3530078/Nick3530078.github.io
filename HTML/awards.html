Phi Beta Kappa is one of the country's most respected honors societies! As an undergraduate, I was inducted into the society due to my excellent academic standing and drive for developing my education.

Summa Cum Laude

At Kent State University, Summa Cum Laude is reserved for only those who graduate with a GPA of 3.9 or higher.

Dean's and President's Lists

Throughout my years at Kent State University, I was placed on the Dean's or President's Lists multiple times! The President's List is reserved for those who achieved a 4.0 GPA on 15 or more credit hours in a single semester. Moreover, the Dean's List is reserved for those who achieved a 3.4 GPA on 12 or more credit hours in a single semester.
